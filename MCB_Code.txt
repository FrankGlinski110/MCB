\cf2 \strokec2 //  CIRCLES & TRIANGLES:\cf4 \strokec4 \
\cf2 \strokec2 //    - LITTLE CIRCLE: They appear at all WaveTrend wave crossings.\cf4 \strokec4 \
\cf2 \strokec2 //    - GREEN CIRCLE: The wavetrend waves are at the oversold level and have crossed up (bullish).\cf4 \strokec4 \
\cf2 \strokec2 //    - RED CIRCLE: The wavetrend waves are at the overbought level and have crossed down (bearish).\cf4 \strokec4 \
\cf2 \strokec2 //    - GOLD/ORANGE CIRCLE: When RSI is below 20, WaveTrend waves are below or equal to -80 and have crossed up after good bullish divergence (DONT BUY WHEN GOLD CIRCLE APPEAR).\cf4 \strokec4 \
\cf2 \strokec2 //    - None of these circles are certain signs to trade. It is only information that can help you. \cf4 \strokec4 \
\cf2 \strokec2 //    - PURPLE TRIANGLE: Appear when a bullish or bearish divergence is formed and WaveTrend waves crosses at overbought and oversold points.\cf4 \strokec4 \
